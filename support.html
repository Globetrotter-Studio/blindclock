Frequently asked questions 常見問題

I can’t hear the level-change sounds. 聽不到級別切換的提示音。
BlindClock’s chimes play even when the iPhone silent switch is on — but they use the media volume. Turn the volume up with the side buttons while the app is open, and check that sounds are enabled in the tournament’s settings. While the app is in the background, alerts are delivered as notifications, so allow notifications when the app asks. BlindClock 的提示音即使在 iPhone 靜音模式下也會播放，但音量取決於媒體音量。請在 App 開啟時用側邊按鈕調高音量，並確認賽事設定中已開啟音效。App 退到背景時，提示改由通知送達，因此請允許 App 的通知權限。
What does BlindClock Pro include, and is it a subscription? BlindClock Pro 包含什麼？是訂閱制嗎？
Pro is a one-time purchase — not a subscription. The free version includes everything you need to run a game, plus up to 5 custom tournaments. Pro removes that limit so you can save unlimited tournaments, on all devices signed in with your Apple Account. Pro 是一次性買斷，不是訂閱。免費版已包含主持牌局所需的全部功能，並可建立最多 5 個自訂賽事；Pro 解除這個上限，讓你儲存無限個賽事，且在使用同一 Apple 帳號的所有裝置上通用。
I bought Pro but it shows as locked. How do I restore my purchase? 已購買 Pro 但顯示未解鎖，如何恢復購買？
Open the upgrade screen (tap the Pro banner on the home screen) and tap “Restore Purchases”. Make sure the device is signed in with the same Apple Account you used to buy Pro. 打開升級頁面（點首頁的 Pro 橫幅），然後點「恢復購買」。請確認裝置登入的 Apple 帳號與當初購買 Pro 的帳號相同。
How does iCloud sync work? iCloud 同步如何運作？
Your custom tournaments sync automatically through your private iCloud account — no setup needed. Both devices must be signed in to the same Apple Account with iCloud Drive enabled. If you’re not signed in to iCloud, the app simply keeps everything on the device. The running clock itself doesn’t sync between devices — only your saved tournaments do. 自訂賽事會透過你的私人 iCloud 帳號自動同步，無需任何設定。兩台裝置必須登入同一個 Apple 帳號並啟用 iCloud 雲碟。如果未登入 iCloud，App 會將所有資料保留在裝置上。注意：同步的是已儲存的賽事範本，執行中的時鐘不會跨裝置同步。
How do I share a tournament with a friend? 如何把賽事分享給朋友？
Swipe left on any tournament in your list and tap the share icon to export it as a .blindclock file — send it via AirDrop, Messages, or any app. When your friend opens the file, BlindClock shows a preview and imports it with one tap. 在賽事列表中向左滑動，點分享圖示即可匯出 .blindclock 檔，透過 AirDrop、訊息或任何 App 傳送。朋友打開檔案後，BlindClock 會顯示預覽，一鍵即可匯入。
The Live Activity doesn’t appear on my Lock Screen. 鎖定畫面上沒有出現 Live Activity。
Check Settings → BlindClock and make sure Live Activities are allowed. You can also toggle the lock-screen banner from the in-app settings while a tournament is running. On iPhone 14 Pro and later, the current level also appears in the Dynamic Island. 請到「設定 → BlindClock」確認已允許「即時動態」。比賽進行中也可以在 App 內設定切換鎖定畫面橫幅。iPhone 14 Pro 及之後的機型，目前級別也會顯示在動態島上。
How do I keep the screen on during a game? 如何讓螢幕在牌局中保持常亮？
Turn on “Keep Screen Awake” in the running tournament’s settings. On iPad in landscape, the table display keeps the screen awake automatically. Plug in the device for long sessions. 在進行中賽事的設定裡開啟「保持螢幕喚醒」。iPad 橫向的牌桌展示模式會自動常亮。長時間牌局建議接上電源。
I force-quit the app mid-tournament. Is my game gone? 比賽中不小心強制關閉 App，進度會不見嗎？
No — BlindClock saves a checkpoint while the clock runs. Reopen the app and it offers to resume the tournament exactly where it left off, with the elapsed time corrected. 不會——時鐘運行期間 BlindClock 會持續保存進度。重新打開 App 即可從中斷的地方繼續比賽，經過的時間也會自動校正。
Which devices are supported? 支援哪些裝置？
BlindClock runs on iPhone and iPad with iOS / iPadOS 18.6 or later, in English and Traditional Chinese. BlindClock 支援 iOS / iPadOS 18.6 以上的 iPhone 與 iPad，提供英文與繁體中文介面。
